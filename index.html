Why Choose Us?

Hello, This is a “No Frills”, Christ-centered ministry, designed to help you feel safe to share your pain, and to feel validated in your trauma. Our objective is to guide you, with God’s help towards healing, restoration & redemption. I know exactly what you are struggling with, because I have been there and done that…I have experienced the same emotional and psychological trauma and pain caused by “marital infidelity and betrayal”….”your story is my story”. The pain and trauma of marital betrayal cuts to the core of a betrayed partner’s soul. It leaves scars that sometimes seem like they take forever to heal, but with God’s help, along with effective strategies and techniques to help you cope, you can move beyond your trauma, to restoration and redemption. Why a mentor?

A Professional Mentor is not to be confused with a therapist or counselor. Of course, many people find healing through these avenues, with someone who is trained in sexual addiction and betrayal trauma. However, the difference of working with a Professional Mentor is that a Mentor is someone who has personally experienced the same trauma and emotional pain that you went through, and had to deal with similar scars that you struggle with. He/She understands the pain of betrayal caused by marital infidelity because he/she has had this experience personally. A mentor helps to break the chain of “deadly intimate deception” and help you to forgive what you cannot forget.

We offer a free “45 minute” phone consult to answer any questions you may have, and to help you figure out if mentoring is right for you.
